DISPLAY:
 -
| MENU NAME       <--- (reverse colors)
| (Up Arr) Menu Title
|  Menu Title
| (Dn Arr) Menu Title
 -

Keys:
Up Arr: menu/value up
Left Arr: prev field
Right Arr: next field
Dn Arr: menu/value down
Check: Select/Save/Show Menu
X: Back/Exit/Clear/Exit Menu (at top level)

When not in menu, any key except Check just triggers backlight
